AirPods Pro 2nd Generation - Technical Specifications

Audio Technology
- Apple H2 chip
- Custom high-excursion Apple driver
- Custom high dynamic range amplifier
- Adaptive EQ
- Adaptive Audio
- Active Noise Cancellation
- Transparency mode
- Conversation Awareness
- Personalized Spatial Audio with dynamic head tracking

Battery Life
- Up to 6 hours of listening time with ANC on (single charge)
- Up to 7 hours with ANC off
- Up to 30 hours total with MagSafe Charging Case
- 5 minutes charge provides around 1 hour of listening time

Charging Case
- MagSafe Charging Case with speaker and lanyard loop
- USB-C connector
- Compatible with Apple Watch charger
- Qi-certified wireless charging
- Built-in speaker for Find My
- IP54 dust, sweat, and water resistant (earbuds and case)

Sensors
- Dual beamforming microphones
- Inward-facing microphone
- Skin-detect sensor
- Motion-detecting accelerometer
- Speech-detecting accelerometer
- Touch control

Controls
- Press once to play, pause, or answer a call
- Press twice to skip forward
- Press three times to skip back
- Press and hold to switch between Active Noise Cancellation and Transparency mode
- Swipe volume control on stem
- "Hey Siri" or just "Siri"

Connectivity
- Bluetooth 5.3
- Find My network support
- Precision Finding with U1 chip in case

Size and Weight
- Earbuds: 30.9 x 21.8 x 24.0 mm, 5.3 grams each
- MagSafe Charging Case: 50.8 x 45.2 x 21.7 mm, 50.8 grams

In the Box
- AirPods Pro (2nd generation)
- MagSafe Charging Case (USB-C)
- Silicone ear tips (four sizes: XS, S, M, L)
- USB-C Charge Cable
- Documentation

Accessibility
- Live Listen audio
- Headphone Accommodations
- Conversation Boost

Operating System Requirements
- iPhone and iPod touch models with iOS 16 or later
- iPad models with iPadOS 16 or later
- Apple Watch with watchOS 9 or later
- Mac with macOS Ventura or later
- Apple TV with tvOS 16 or later

Price
- $249

Release Date
- September 2023 (USB-C version)

Key Features
- Up to 2x more Active Noise Cancellation
- Adaptive Audio dynamically adjusts noise control
- Personalized Spatial Audio
- USB-C charging
- Precision Finding
- Dust, sweat, and water resistant (IP54)
