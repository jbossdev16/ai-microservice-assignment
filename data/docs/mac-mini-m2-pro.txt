Mac Mini M2 Pro - Technical Specifications

Chip
- Apple M2 Pro chip
- 10-core CPU or 12-core CPU with up to 8 performance cores and 4 efficiency cores
- 16-core GPU or 19-core GPU
- 16-core Neural Engine
- 200GB/s memory bandwidth

Storage
- Configurable to 512GB, 1TB, 2TB, 4TB, or 8TB SSD

Memory
- 16GB or 32GB unified memory

Ports and Connectivity
Front:
- Two Thunderbolt 4 (USB-C) ports

Back:
- Two Thunderbolt 4 (USB-C) ports
- Two USB-A ports
- One HDMI port
- One Gigabit Ethernet port (configurable to 10Gb Ethernet)
- One 3.5 mm headphone jack

Wireless:
- Wi-Fi 6E (802.11ax)
- Bluetooth 5.3

Display Support
- Supports up to three displays
- Two displays with up to 6K resolution at 60Hz over Thunderbolt and one display with up to 4K resolution at 144Hz over HDMI

Audio
- 3.5 mm headphone jack with advanced support for high-impedance headphones

Power
- 150W power supply

Design
- Silver aluminum
- Dimensions: 19.7 x 19.7 x 3.6 cm
- Weight: 1.3 kg

Operating System
- macOS Sonoma

Price
- Starting at $1,299

Release Date
- January 2023

Key Features
- M2 Pro chip for powerful performance
- Compact design that fits anywhere
- Extensive connectivity with Thunderbolt 4
- Supports up to three displays
- Configurable Ethernet up to 10Gb
- Energy efficient
