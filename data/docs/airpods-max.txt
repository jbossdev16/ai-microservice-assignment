AirPods Max - Technical Specifications

Audio Technology
- Apple H1 chip in each ear cup
- Custom acoustic design
- 40 mm Apple-designed dynamic driver
- Active Noise Cancellation
- Transparency mode
- Adaptive EQ
- Spatial Audio with dynamic head tracking
- Computational audio

Battery Life
- Up to 20 hours of listening time with ANC or Transparency mode enabled
- Up to 20 hours of movie playback with spatial audio enabled
- Up to 20 hours of talk time with ANC enabled
- 5 minutes charge provides around 1.5 hours of listening time

Charging
- Lightning connector for charging
- Smart Case for ultra-low-power state

Sensors
- Nine microphones total
- Eight microphones for Active Noise Cancellation
- Three microphones for voice pickup
- Optical sensor (each ear cup)
- Position sensor (each ear cup)
- Case-detect sensor (each ear cup)
- Accelerometer (left ear cup)
- Gyroscope (left ear cup)

Controls
- Digital Crown for volume control, play/pause, skip tracks, answer/end calls, and activate Siri
- Noise control button to switch between Active Noise Cancellation and Transparency mode
- "Hey Siri" or just "Siri"

Connectivity
- Bluetooth 5.0
- Apple H1 chip for seamless device switching

Design
- Breathable knit mesh canopy
- Memory foam ear cushions
- Stainless steel frame and headband
- Telescoping arms
- Available in Space Gray, Silver, Pink, Green, Sky Blue

Size and Weight
- Height: 187.3 mm
- Width: 168.6 mm
- Depth: 83.4 mm
- Weight: 384.8 grams

In the Box
- AirPods Max
- Smart Case
- Lightning to USB-C Cable
- Documentation

Accessibility
- Live Listen audio
- Headphone Accommodations
- Announce Notifications

Operating System Requirements
- iPhone and iPod touch models with iOS 14.3 or later
- iPad models with iPadOS 14.3 or later
- Apple Watch with watchOS 7.2 or later
- Mac with macOS Big Sur 11.1 or later
- Apple TV with tvOS 14.3 or later

Price
- $549

Release Date
- December 2020

Key Features
- High-fidelity audio with computational audio
- Adaptive EQ for sound optimization
- Active Noise Cancellation with Transparency mode
- Spatial Audio with dynamic head tracking
- Premium materials and design
- Up to 20 hours of battery life
- Digital Crown for precise control
- Seamless switching between Apple devices
