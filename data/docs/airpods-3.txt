AirPods 3rd Generation - Technical Specifications

Audio Technology
- Apple H1 chip
- Custom high-excursion Apple driver
- Custom high dynamic range amplifier
- Adaptive EQ
- Spatial Audio with dynamic head tracking
- Computational audio

Battery Life
- Up to 6 hours of listening time (single charge)
- Up to 4 hours of talk time (single charge)
- Up to 30 hours total with MagSafe Charging Case
- 5 minutes charge provides around 1 hour of listening time

Charging Case
- MagSafe Charging Case
- Lightning connector
- Qi-certified wireless charging compatible
- IPX4 sweat and water resistant (earbuds and case)

Sensors
- Dual beamforming microphones
- Inward-facing microphone
- Skin-detect sensor
- Motion-detecting accelerometer
- Speech-detecting accelerometer

Controls
- Press once to play, pause, or answer a call
- Press twice to skip forward
- Press three times to skip back
- Press and hold for Siri
- "Hey Siri" or just "Siri"

Connectivity
- Bluetooth 5.0
- Automatic device switching
- Audio Sharing between two sets of AirPods

Size and Weight
- Earbuds: 30.79 x 18.26 x 19.21 mm, 4.28 grams each
- MagSafe Charging Case: 46.40 x 54.40 x 21.38 mm, 37.91 grams

In the Box
- AirPods (3rd generation)
- MagSafe Charging Case
- Lightning to USB-C Cable
- Documentation

Accessibility
- Live Listen audio
- Headphone Accommodations
- Announce Notifications

Operating System Requirements
- iPhone and iPod touch models with iOS 15.1 or later
- iPad models with iPadOS 15.1 or later
- Apple Watch with watchOS 8.1 or later
- Mac with macOS Monterey 12.1 or later
- Apple TV with tvOS 15.1 or later

Price
- $169

Release Date
- October 2021

Key Features
- Spatial Audio with dynamic head tracking
- Adaptive EQ for personalized sound
- Longer battery life than previous generation
- Sweat and water resistant (IPX4)
- MagSafe charging
- Force sensor for easy control
- Contoured design for comfortable fit
- Automatic switching between devices
